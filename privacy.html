Privacy Policy for [Livesoon-Timer] Last Updated: [14/11/2025] This Privacy Policy explains how [Livesoon-Timer] "we" collects, uses, and discloses your information. 1. Information We Collect When you authorize our App, we collect the following information from TikTok: TikTok User ID: We use this to identify your account and associate it with your timer settings. TikTok Access Token: We collect and securely store this "key" to make API calls on your behalf. Its only use is to send your pre-defined chat notification (using the live.chat.send permission) when your timer ends. We DO NOT collect or store your TikTok password, email address, follower lists, or any other personal information. 2. How We Use Your Information Your information is used only for the following purposes: To provide the core service of the App. To securely send your one-time chat notification to your own LIVE stream at your request. To save your settings (like themes and custom text) for your next stream. 3. How We Store and Protect Your Information Your TikTok User ID and Access Token are stored securely in an encrypted database. We use industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access. 4. Information Sharing We DO NOT and will NEVER share, sell, rent, or trade your information with any third parties for marketing or any other purpose. The only time your information is used is for the direct, server-to-server communication with the TikTok API to post your message. 5. Data Deletion You can revoke the App's permissions at any time via your TikTok account settings.
